How to make scrambled eggs

description

scrambled eggs are a very popular breakfast dish. they are very easy to make and you can make them right at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl
  2. Add milk, salt, and pepper
  3. Whisk until the mixture is smooth
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low heat
  5. Add the butter and let it melt
  6. Pour the egg mixture into the pan
  7. Gently stir with a spatula until the eggs begin to set
  8. Continue stirring until the eggs are soft and fluffy
  9. Remove from heat before they become dry
  10. Then serve and enjoy

Cooking Tips

Cook the eggs on low heat and stir gently for the fluffiest texture. Avoid overcooking because the eggs will continue cooking after you remove them from the pan.
