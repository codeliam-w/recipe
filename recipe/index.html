How to Make Scrambled Eggs

About

Scrambled eggs are a quick and easy breakfast. They only take a few minutes to make and taste great.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Mix them with a fork.
  3. Melt the butter in a pan.
  4. Pour in the eggs.
  5. Stir until the eggs are cooked.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
